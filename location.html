Why Use a QR Code for Location?

Location QR codes eliminate the frustration of typing addresses or searching for businesses. When scanned, the user's phone opens their default map app with your location already pinned. They can immediately get directions, see business hours, or call you directly from the map interface.

These QR codes are particularly valuable for businesses in hard-to-find locations, pop-up shops, food trucks that move daily, and event venues. Real estate agents use them on property flyers, while restaurants add them to online ordering confirmations for pickup customers.

How It Works

Search for your business on Google Maps, click 'Share', and copy the link. Alternatively, search your address and copy the URL from the browser. Paste it into the generator above, click generate, and download your QR code. When scanned, it opens your location in any map app.

Best Practices

  • Use a Google Maps short URL for a cleaner, more scannable QR code
  • Place on storefront windows so passersby can save your location
  • Include on business cards for out-of-town clients and partners
  • Test with both Google Maps and Apple Maps on different phones

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code for my business location?
Search for your business on Google Maps, click the 'Share' button, and copy the link. Paste this URL into the PlainQR generator and create your code. It will open directly in any map app when scanned.
Will the QR code open Google Maps or Apple Maps?
It opens the user's default map app. On Android, it typically opens Google Maps. On iPhone, it opens Apple Maps. Both will show your location and provide directions.
Can I use a QR code for a moving location like a food truck?
Yes, but you'll need to update the QR code daily with your new location. Consider creating a simple webpage with your current location and linking the QR code to that page instead.
What if my business has multiple locations?
Create a separate QR code for each location. Alternatively, link to a 'Locations' page on your website where customers can choose the nearest branch.
